Join the Inland Valley Chamber of Commerce!

Membership Levels

Non-Profit Membership

Price: Free!

The Non-Profit Membership provides access to membership of the chamber with just one spotlight per year. The goodnews? The Non-Profit Membership is free!

Bronze Membership Plan

Price: $250 per year

The Bronze Membership Plan gets businesses and startups a 10% discount on use of the Chamber of Commerce building for functions they would like to hold, including 3 spotlights and one news release on the Chamber of Commerce website per year.

The Silver Membership Plan

Price: $350 per year

The Silver Membership Plan provides a 15% discount on use of the Chamber of Commerce building for functions, and 4 spotlights on the Chamber of Commerce website per year, with the choice of what month your business is featured!

Gold Membership Plan

Price: $600 per year

The Gold Membership Plan provides a 25% discount on use of the Chamber of Commerce building and 6 spotlights, with the member's choice of what month the spotlight is featured!
